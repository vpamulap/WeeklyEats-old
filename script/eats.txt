Green-Grocer Goulash

1 10 oz can mixed vegetables
1 15 oz can barbecue baked beans
1 10 oz can vegetable broth
1 12 oz bag no yolk egg noodles
1 tbsp paprika

Cook egg noodles according to package directions. Dump the vegetables, beans, broth, and paprika into a large saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Cover and cook over low heat for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve over the prepared noodles.

Border-Patrol Casserole

2 5 oz can chunk lean ham, drained
0.5 cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
2 15 oz can barbecue baked beans
1 8 oz chunky salsa
1.5 cups baked tortilla chips
1.5 tsp oregano

Preheat the oven to 350F. Dump the ham, beans and salsa into a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish. Smother with the chips, cheese, and oregano. Bake for 30 minutes, until the cheese melts and the casserole is heated through.

Tijuana Mac And Beef

1 15 oz can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1 lb extra-lean ground beef
2 14 oz can zesty diced tomatoes with chiles
1 cup elbow macaroni
0.25 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp taco seasoning
0.5 cup shredded reduced fat cheddar cheese

Brown the beef and garlic powder in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain off the grease. Dump in the beans, tomatoes (with juice), macaroni, and taco seasoning. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally. Top with the cheese.

Ham-And-’Shroom Scramble

1 5 oz can chunk lean ham, drained
1 6 oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
2 eggs, beaten
4 egg whites, beaten
0.5 cup shredded low-fat mozzarella cheese
1 15 oz can sliced new potatoes, drained
0.25 cup fat-free milk
0.25 tsp ground black pepper

Preheat the broiler. Coat a medium nonstick skillet with cooking spray. Dump in the ham, mushrooms, and potatoes. Cook over medium heat. In a bowl, mix the eggs, milk, and pepper. Pour into the skillet and cook, stirring once or twice, until the eggs begin to set. Cook undisturbed for 3 minutes, until the eggs are set in the middle but jiggly on top. Top with the cheese and run under the broiler for 2 minutes, until puffy and golden.

‘50s-Style Creamed Chicken

1 10 oz can chunk chicken breast, drained and flaked
1 11 oz can reduced-fat cream of mushroom soup
1 6 oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 bag 12 oz no yolk egg noodles
0.5 cup chopped onion
2 tsp olive oil
1 cup fat-free sour cream

Cook egg noodles according to package directions. Cook the onion in the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, until tender. Dump in the chicken and stir. Cook for 1 minute. Stir in the soup and mushrooms. Simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in the sour cream over low heat. Heat through and serve over the cooked noodles.
